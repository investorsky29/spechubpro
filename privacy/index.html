Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SpecHub (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at spechubpro.com.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect usage data (pages visited, browser type, time spent), contact information submitted voluntarily through our contact form or newsletter, and cookies for preferences such as dark mode and compare list state.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to operate and improve SpecHub, respond to contact form submissions, send newsletters to subscribers who opt in, analyse usage patterns, and serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

3. Third-Party Advertising

SpecHub displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps by visiting the Google Privacy & Terms page. You can also manage your advertising preferences through Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's previous visits to this website and other websites.

4. Cookies

SpecHub uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, remember your preferences, analyse website traffic, and display relevant advertisements. Some cookies are essential for the website to function properly, while others are used for analytics and advertising. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features of the website may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with analytics providers, email service providers for newsletters, and hosting providers — all bound by their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at suppor@spechubpro.com.

8. Children's Privacy

SpecHub is intended for a general audience and is not directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can remove the information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of SpecHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy: Contact Us or email hello@spechubpro.com.
