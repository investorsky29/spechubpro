Our Story

SpecHub was built out of frustration with scattered, incomplete smartphone information online. Buyers in African markets often relied on global websites that ignored locally popular brands like Tecno, Infinix, and itel.

We set out to build a database that treats every brand with equal depth — whether you are researching a $149 itel S25 Pro or a $1,299 Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra, you deserve the same quality of information.

Why Trust SpecHub?

  • Specifications sourced directly from manufacturer press releases and official pages
  • Reviews written by experienced technology journalists with hands-on device time
  • No paid placements — rankings reflect genuine editorial assessment
  • Regular database updates as new phones launch
  • Transparent methodology for all ratings and scores
