Expert Review

iPhone 17 Air is Apple boldest iPhone design statement since the original. At 5.5mm thin and 145g it is simultaneously the slimmest and lightest iPhone ever made. Apple A18 ensures no performance compromise despite the wafer-thin profile, and Apple Intelligence V2 brings the same on-device AI as the Pro models. The single 48MP rear camera is excellent in daylight and low light alike. For users who prioritise pocketability and aesthetics over telephoto or battery capacity, nothing in the world comes close.
