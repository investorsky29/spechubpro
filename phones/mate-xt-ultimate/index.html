Expert Review

Mate XT Ultimate Design is Huawei's most ambitious smartphone, introducing the world's first commercially available tri-fold display. Its innovative OLED panel transforms from a standard 6.4-inch smartphone into a massive 10.2-inch tablet, delivering an unmatched multitasking and entertainment experience. Powered by the Kirin 9010 chipset and equipped with 16GB of RAM, the device handles productivity and everyday tasks smoothly. Huawei's XMAGE triple-camera system features a 50MP variable-aperture main sensor, ultrawide lens, and 5.5x periscope telephoto camera for versatile photography. Despite its ultra-thin foldable design, it houses a 5600mAh battery with 66W wired and 50W wireless charging. The Mate XT Ultimate is a showcase of cutting-edge foldable engineering, though global users should note the lack of Google Mobile Services on the international version.
