Expert Review

The Nokia C300 is an affordable entry-level smartphone designed for everyday communication, web browsing, and media consumption. It features a 6.52-inch HD+ IPS LCD display and is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 662 processor, delivering dependable performance for daily tasks. The triple rear camera system includes a 13MP main camera alongside macro and depth sensors, while the 8MP front camera is suitable for selfies and video calls. A 4000mAh battery provides all-day battery life, and the phone includes expandable storage via microSD, a rear-mounted fingerprint sensor, a USB Type-C port, and a 3.5mm headphone jack. Running Android 12, the Nokia C300 offers a clean and user-friendly software experience, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious users.
