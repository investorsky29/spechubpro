Expert Review

The realme P4 Power 5G is one of the world's first smartphones to feature a massive 10,001mAh silicon-carbon battery while maintaining a relatively slim design. Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7400 Ultra processor, it offers excellent everyday performance alongside a vibrant 6.8-inch 1.5K 144Hz AMOLED display. The phone supports 80W SUPERVOOC fast charging and 27W reverse wired charging, allowing it to function as a power bank for other devices. With IP66/IP68/IP69 protection, Android 16, stereo speakers, and outstanding battery endurance, the P4 Power is built for users who prioritize long-lasting performance.
