Expert Review

The vivo iQOO Neo 10R is a performance-focused smartphone designed for gamers and power users. Powered by Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 chipset, it delivers flagship-level gaming and multitasking performance while maintaining excellent power efficiency. The 6.78-inch 1.5K AMOLED display supports a 144Hz refresh rate in supported games, providing smooth visuals and responsive gameplay. Photography is handled by a 50MP Sony IMX882 main camera with OIS alongside an 8MP ultrawide camera, while the 32MP front camera captures detailed selfies. A massive 6400mAh battery easily lasts a full day of heavy use and supports 80W FlashCharge for quick top-ups. With LPDDR5X RAM, fast UFS storage, stereo speakers, and Android 15, the iQOO Neo 10R offers outstanding value for users seeking flagship-grade performance at a mid-range price.
