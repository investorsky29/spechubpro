Expert Review

iPhone 16e brings Apple's flagship A18 chip to a more affordable price point without sacrificing everyday performance. The 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display delivers vibrant colors and sharp visuals, while the 48MP Fusion camera captures detailed photos and supports 2x optical-quality zoom. Apple Intelligence features, USB-C connectivity, and excellent battery life make it a strong value for users entering the Apple ecosystem. Although it features only a single rear camera and a 60Hz display, the iPhone 16e offers the smooth software experience, premium build quality, and long software support that define the iPhone lineup.
