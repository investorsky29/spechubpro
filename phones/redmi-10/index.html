Expert Review

The Xiaomi Redmi 10 is a well-balanced budget smartphone that offers features usually found in more expensive devices. It sports a 6.5-inch Full HD+ IPS LCD with a smooth 90Hz AdaptiveSync refresh rate, making scrolling and gaming more fluid. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G88 chipset, it delivers reliable performance for everyday tasks, casual gaming, and multimedia. The quad-camera setup includes a 50MP main sensor, an 8MP ultrawide camera, a 2MP macro camera, and a 2MP depth sensor, allowing for versatile photography. A 5000mAh battery provides excellent battery life and supports 18W fast charging. Additional highlights include dual stereo speakers, a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, expandable storage, an infrared blaster, and a 3.5mm headphone jack, making the Redmi 10 one of the best-value budget smartphones of 2021.
