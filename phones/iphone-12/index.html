Expert Review

The Apple iPhone 12 marked the beginning of Apple's modern flat-edge design and remains a solid choice years after its release. Powered by the A14 Bionic chip, it delivers excellent performance for gaming, photography, and everyday tasks while continuing to receive the latest iOS updates. Its 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display produces vibrant colors, deep blacks, and outstanding brightness. The dual 12MP camera system captures sharp photos and records high-quality Dolby Vision HDR videos, while MagSafe support adds convenient wireless charging and accessories. With IP68 water resistance, premium build quality, and long-term software support, the iPhone 12 continues to offer excellent value.
