Expert Review

The Infinix Smart 8 is an affordable smartphone built for everyday use, offering dependable performance and a modern design. Powered by the Unisoc T606 processor, it handles social media, messaging, web browsing, and light gaming with ease. The 6.6-inch IPS LCD features a smooth 90Hz refresh rate for fluid scrolling, while the 13MP AI camera captures decent photos in daylight. A large 5000mAh battery provides all-day battery life, and the phone also includes expandable storage, USB-C charging, a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, and Android 13 with XOS 13. It is an excellent choice for users seeking a reliable entry-level smartphone at an affordable price.
