Expert Review

The Tecno Pova 7 is a gaming-focused budget smartphone designed for users who need exceptional battery life and dependable everyday performance. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G100 Ultimate chipset, it delivers smooth multitasking and responsive gaming, while the 6.78-inch Full HD+ IPS display with a 120Hz refresh rate ensures fluid scrolling and gameplay. Its 108MP AI camera captures detailed photos, and the massive 7000mAh battery easily provides up to two days of usage with support for 45W Flash Charge and 10W reverse charging. Running Android 15 with HiOS 15, the Pova 7 also includes IP64 dust and splash resistance, NFC, an IR blaster, stereo speakers, and Tecno's FreeLink communication feature, making it one of the strongest battery-focused smartphones in its class.
