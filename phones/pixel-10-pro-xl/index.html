Expert Review

Pixel 10 Pro XL is Google's most advanced smartphone, combining premium hardware with industry-leading AI features. Powered by the Tensor G5 chip, it delivers exceptional on-device AI capabilities, including advanced photo editing, real-time translation, call screening, and Gemini-powered productivity tools. The 6.8-inch LTPO OLED display offers vibrant colors, excellent brightness, and a smooth adaptive 120Hz refresh rate. Its versatile triple-camera system remains one of the best in the industry, producing stunning photos, excellent low-light shots, and reliable 5x optical zoom results. With a large 5300mAh battery, wireless charging, IP68 durability, and seven years of software updates, the Pixel 10 Pro XL is one of the best Android flagships for photography and AI features.
