Expert Review

The itel P70 is built for users who value exceptional battery life without sacrificing everyday performance. Its massive 6000mAh battery easily delivers up to two days of typical use, making it ideal for streaming, gaming, and extended productivity. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G50 Ultimate processor, the phone handles daily tasks smoothly, while the 6.67-inch 120Hz display provides a fluid viewing experience. The 13MP AI camera captures clear photos for social media, and Android 14 delivers a clean and responsive software experience. With IP54 splash resistance, expandable storage, USB-C charging, and excellent endurance, the itel P70 stands out as one of the best budget battery smartphones available.
