Expert Review

The Tecno Camon 50 Ultra is Tecno's most advanced camera-focused flagship smartphone. Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 8350 Ultimate chipset, it delivers flagship-level performance for gaming, multitasking, and AI-powered photography. The stunning 6.78-inch LTPO AMOLED display supports an adaptive refresh rate of up to 144Hz for smooth visuals while improving battery efficiency. Its professional-grade triple-camera setup combines a 50MP Sony LYT-900 main sensor, a 50MP ultrawide camera, and a 50MP periscope telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom, making it one of the strongest camera systems in its class. A large 6000mAh battery with 70W wired and 30W wireless charging keeps the phone powered throughout the day. With IP68/IP69 durability, Dolby Atmos stereo speakers, and Android 16 with HiOS 16, the Camon 50 Ultra delivers a true flagship experience at a competitive price.
