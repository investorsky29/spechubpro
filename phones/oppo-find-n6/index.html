Expert Review

The OPPO Find N6 is OPPO's most advanced foldable smartphone, combining an ultra-thin design with flagship performance and industry-leading AI features. Its 8.12-inch LTPO AMOLED inner display and 6.62-inch cover display both support adaptive 1-120Hz refresh rates, while the second-generation Titanium Flexion Hinge delivers a nearly invisible crease for a seamless viewing experience. Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 platform with 16GB of LPDDR5X RAM, the Find N6 excels in multitasking, gaming, and AI-powered productivity. The Hasselblad camera system features a 200MP main sensor alongside 50MP ultrawide and 50MP periscope telephoto cameras, producing exceptional photos and videos. A large 6000mAh silicon-carbon battery supports 80W SUPERVOOC wired charging and 50W AIRVOOC wireless charging. With Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6.0, IP56/IP58/IP59 water resistance, OPPO AI Pen support, and Android 16 with ColorOS 16, the Find N6 stands among the world's best foldable smartphones.
