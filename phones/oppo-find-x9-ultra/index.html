Expert Review

The OPPO Find X9 Ultra is OPPO's most advanced flagship smartphone, combining cutting-edge performance with one of the best mobile camera systems available. Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor, it delivers exceptional speed for gaming, AI tasks, photography, and multitasking. Its 6.82-inch QHD+ LTPO AMOLED display supports adaptive refresh rates from 1Hz to 120Hz, with up to 144Hz in supported games, offering vibrant colors and smooth visuals. The Hasselblad-tuned quad-camera system features a 200MP main sensor, a 50MP ultrawide camera, a 200MP periscope telephoto lens, and a 50MP ultra-telephoto camera, enabling outstanding photography and up to 10x optical zoom. A huge 7050mAh silicon-carbon battery supports 100W SUPERVOOC wired charging and 50W AIRVOOC wireless charging. Running Android 16 with ColorOS 16, the Find X9 Ultra also includes Wi-Fi 7, IP68/IP69 durability, an ultrasonic in-display fingerprint scanner, and seven years of software support, making it one of the most complete Android flagships on the market today.
