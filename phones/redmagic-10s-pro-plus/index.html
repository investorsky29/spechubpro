Expert Review

RedMagic 10S Pro+ is RedMagic's ultimate gaming flagship, combining desktop-class performance with one of the largest batteries ever fitted inside a premium smartphone. The Snapdragon 8 Elite Leading Version, paired with the RedCore R3 Pro gaming chip and the advanced ICE-X liquid metal cooling system, delivers exceptional sustained performance for even the most demanding games. Its immersive 6.85-inch 144Hz AMOLED display features ultra-thin bezels and an under-display selfie camera for uninterrupted gameplay. The huge 7500mAh battery easily powers extended gaming sessions and recharges rapidly with 120W fast charging. Combined with responsive shoulder triggers, RGB lighting, stereo speakers, and gamer-focused software, the RedMagic 10S Pro+ stands among the most powerful gaming smartphones available.
