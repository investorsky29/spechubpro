Expert Review

The Infinix Hot 40 is a well-rounded budget smartphone designed for users who want a smooth display and dependable performance without spending much. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G88 processor, it handles multitasking, gaming, and entertainment comfortably. Its large 6.78-inch Full HD+ display features a 120Hz refresh rate for fluid scrolling and gameplay, while the 50MP AI camera captures detailed daylight photos. The 5000mAh battery easily lasts an entire day and supports 33W FastCharge for quick recharging. Combined with stereo speakers, Magic Ring notifications, and Android 13 with XOS 13.5, the Hot 40 delivers excellent value in the budget segment.
