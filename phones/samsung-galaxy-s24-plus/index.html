Expert Review

The Samsung Galaxy S24+ brings flagship hardware together with Galaxy AI features and long-term software support. It features a stunning 6.7-inch QHD+ Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with an adaptive 1-120Hz refresh rate, a powerful Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 for Galaxy or Exynos 2400 processor depending on the region, and a versatile 50MP triple-camera system. The 4900mAh battery provides excellent endurance, while wireless charging, IP68 durability, and Samsung's promise of seven years of software updates make the Galaxy S24+ one of the best Android flagships available.
