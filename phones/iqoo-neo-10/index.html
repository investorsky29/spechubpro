Expert Review

The vivo iQOO Neo 10 is a performance-focused smartphone designed for gamers and power users. It combines the Snapdragon 8s Gen 4 chipset with up to 16GB of LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.1 storage, delivering flagship-level speed at a lower price. The 6.78-inch AMOLED display offers a smooth 144Hz refresh rate with exceptional brightness, making it ideal for gaming and multimedia. Its 50MP Sony main camera with OIS captures detailed photos, while the massive 7000mAh battery provides outstanding endurance and supports ultra-fast 120W FlashCharge. With Wi-Fi 7, stereo speakers, IP65 protection, and Android 15, the iQOO Neo 10 is one of the best gaming smartphones in its price segment.
