Expert Review

OnePlus 15 continues the brand's flagship-killer tradition by combining premium hardware with fast, clean software. Powered by Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, it delivers outstanding performance for gaming, productivity, and AI-powered features. The stunning 6.82-inch 2K LTPO AMOLED display offers adaptive 1-120Hz refresh rates, exceptional brightness, and vibrant colors. Co-developed with Hasselblad, the versatile triple-camera system captures detailed photos and impressive portraits, while the large 6500mAh silicon-carbon battery easily lasts a full day and recharges rapidly with 100W SUPERVOOC and 50W AIRVOOC wireless charging. Premium build quality, IP68 durability, and OxygenOS make the OnePlus 15 one of the best flagship Android smartphones available.
