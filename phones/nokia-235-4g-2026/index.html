Expert Review

The HMD Nokia 235 4G (2026) is a modern feature phone that combines Nokia's classic durability with updated connectivity and AI features. It features a 2.8-inch IPS display, a physical keypad, and 4G VoLTE support for clear voice calls. Powered by the Unisoc T107 chipset with 64MB RAM, it handles calling, texting, FM radio, Bluetooth, and basic apps with ease. A new dedicated AI button provides quick access to voice-assisted tasks, while the removable 1450mAh battery delivers up to 12 days of standby time. Other highlights include a 2MP rear camera with LED flash, a VGA front camera, USB-C charging, Bluetooth 5.0, expandable storage up to 32GB, and the iconic Snake game, making it an excellent backup phone or digital detox device. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
