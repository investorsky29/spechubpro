Expert Review

The Infinix Hot 70 is a feature-rich budget smartphone that combines excellent battery life, reliable performance, and a modern design. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G100 Ultimate processor, it delivers smooth multitasking, responsive gaming, and efficient everyday performance. The large 6.78-inch 120Hz IPS LCD offers fluid scrolling and an immersive viewing experience, while the 50MP AI camera captures detailed photos in various lighting conditions. A massive 6000mAh battery easily lasts up to two days on a single charge and supports 45W FastCharge for quick top-ups. Running Android 16 with XOS 16, the Hot 70 also features dual stereo speakers, IP65 dust and water resistance, NFC, expandable storage, and USB-C connectivity, making it one of the strongest budget smartphones in its category.
