Expert Review

The Motorola Razr 60 Ultra is Motorola's most advanced flip-style foldable smartphone, combining flagship performance with a refined folding design. Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, it delivers exceptional speed for gaming, multitasking, and AI-powered experiences. The 7.0-inch LTPO AMOLED inner display and 4.0-inch cover display both support refresh rates up to 165Hz, providing an incredibly smooth experience whether the phone is open or closed. Its dual 50MP camera system captures detailed photos and 8K video, while Moto AI introduces intelligent productivity and photography features. The 4700mAh battery supports 68W TurboPower wired charging, 30W wireless charging, and 5W reverse charging. With IP48 protection, Wi-Fi 7, Android 15, and premium materials, the Razr 60 Ultra stands among the best flip foldables available.
