Expert Review

Xiaomi 16 Pro delivers a flagship experience with powerful performance, premium design, and impressive battery life. Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite 2 processor, it handles demanding games, multitasking, and AI features effortlessly. The stunning 6.73-inch LTPO AMOLED display offers exceptional brightness, sharp resolution, and adaptive 120Hz refresh rates for a smooth viewing experience. Its Leica-engineered triple-camera system captures detailed photos with natural colors and excellent dynamic range, while the large 6100mAh battery paired with 90W wired and 50W wireless HyperCharge ensures all-day power with minimal downtime. With IP68 durability and HyperOS optimizations, the Xiaomi 16 Pro is one of the strongest premium Android flagships available.
