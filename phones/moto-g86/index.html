Expert Review

The Motorola Moto G86 5G is a premium mid-range smartphone designed to deliver a flagship-like experience at an affordable price. It features a vibrant 6.67-inch 1.5K AMOLED display with a smooth 120Hz refresh rate and up to 4500 nits peak brightness for excellent outdoor visibility. Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7300 chipset, the Moto G86 handles everyday tasks, gaming, and multitasking with ease. Its 50MP Sony LYT-600 main camera with optical image stabilization captures detailed photos and stable videos, while the 5200mAh battery easily lasts all day and supports 30W TurboPower charging. Additional highlights include IP68/IP69 water and dust resistance, MIL-STD-810H durability, stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos, expandable storage via microSD, and Android 15, making it one of Motorola's strongest mid-range smartphones of 2025.
