Expert Review

The OPPO Find X9s is a compact flagship smartphone designed for users who want top-tier performance in a smaller form factor. Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 9500+ processor, it delivers outstanding speed for gaming, multitasking, and AI-powered features. Its 6.32-inch LTPO AMOLED display offers a sharp 1.5K resolution, adaptive 1-120Hz refresh rate, and exceptional brightness for an immersive viewing experience. The Hasselblad-tuned triple-camera system combines a 50MP main sensor, a 50MP ultrawide camera, and a 50MP periscope telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom to capture excellent photos in any situation. A large 6500mAh silicon-carbon battery supports 80W SUPERVOOC wired charging and 50W AIRVOOC wireless charging for impressive endurance. Running Android 16 with ColorOS 16, the Find X9s also features Wi-Fi 7, an ultrasonic fingerprint scanner, IP68/IP69 durability, and premium craftsmanship, making it one of the best compact Android flagships available.
