Expert Review

The Apple iPhone 12 Pro combines premium materials with flagship performance, making it one of Apple's most refined smartphones. Powered by the A14 Bionic chip, it delivers outstanding speed for gaming, photography, and professional workloads while maintaining excellent efficiency. The 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display produces vibrant colors, deep blacks, and impressive brightness. Its professional triple-camera system adds a dedicated telephoto lens and LiDAR Scanner for faster autofocus and enhanced AR experiences, while Apple ProRAW support provides greater editing flexibility. With MagSafe charging, IP68 water resistance, and years of iOS updates, the iPhone 12 Pro remains an excellent premium smartphone.
