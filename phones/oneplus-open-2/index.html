Expert Review

OnePlus Open 2 is OnePlus' most advanced foldable smartphone, combining an ultra-thin premium design with flagship-level performance. Powered by Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, it delivers exceptional speed for multitasking, gaming, and AI-powered features. The large 8.12-inch LTPO AMOLED inner display and bright cover screen provide an immersive viewing experience with adaptive 120Hz refresh rates. Co-engineered with Hasselblad, the versatile triple-camera system captures excellent photos and detailed zoom shots. Its 5900mAh silicon-carbon battery offers outstanding endurance while supporting 80W SUPERVOOC wired and 50W AIRVOOC wireless charging. With Open Canvas multitasking, premium materials, and refined OxygenOS software, the OnePlus Open 2 stands among the best foldable smartphones available.
