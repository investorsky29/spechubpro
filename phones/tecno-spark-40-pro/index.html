Tecno Spark 40 Pro offers an excellent balance between affordability and performance. The 50MP AI camera captures detailed photos, and the 5200mAh battery with 45W fast charging easily powers through a full day. Stereo speakers, Android 15, and modern design make it one of the strongest phones in the budget segment.
