Expert Review

The realme C73 5G is an affordable smartphone designed for users who want reliable 5G connectivity, long battery life, and a smooth display. Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 chipset, it delivers dependable everyday performance, while the 6.67-inch 120Hz display makes scrolling and gaming feel fluid. The 6000mAh battery comfortably lasts a full day or more, and Android 15 with realme UI provides a clean user experience. Expandable storage, IP64 protection, and military-grade durability make the realme C73 an excellent budget 5G device.
