Expert Review

iPhone 16 Plus is designed for users who want a larger display and exceptional battery life without paying Pro-level prices. Powered by Apple's A18 chip, it delivers fast performance for gaming, multitasking, and Apple Intelligence features while maintaining excellent power efficiency. The expansive 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display offers vibrant colors and sharp detail, making it ideal for watching videos, gaming, and productivity. Its 48MP Fusion camera captures stunning photos and supports 2x optical-quality zoom, while the larger 4674mAh battery easily lasts through a full day of heavy use. With IP68 durability, USB-C, MagSafe charging, and years of iOS updates, the iPhone 16 Plus remains one of the best large smartphones in its class.
