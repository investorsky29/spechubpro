Expert Review

The Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra 5G was Samsung's most ambitious flagship of 2020, introducing the first 108MP camera in the Galaxy S series and the iconic 100x Space Zoom. It features a massive 6.9-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with a smooth 120Hz refresh rate, the Snapdragon 865 or Exynos 990 chipset depending on the region, and a large 5000mAh battery with 45W fast charging. The premium design, IP68 water resistance, expandable storage, and versatile quad-camera system make the Galaxy S20 Ultra a capable flagship even years after its release.
