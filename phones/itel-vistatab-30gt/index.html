Expert Review

The itel VistaTab 30GT is an affordable Android tablet built for entertainment, productivity, and light gaming. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G99 Ultimate processor, it offers a noticeable performance boost over entry-level tablets, making multitasking, gaming, and media consumption much smoother. Its 11-inch Full HD+ IPS display with a 90Hz refresh rate provides sharp visuals and fluid scrolling, while the 7000mAh battery delivers all-day battery life. Running Android 15, the tablet also supports a stylus, expandable storage up to 512GB, dual stereo speakers, and 4G LTE connectivity, making it an excellent choice for students, professionals, and casual gamers looking for great value.
