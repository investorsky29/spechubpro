Expert Review

The Google Pixel 8 delivers flagship camera quality, powerful AI features, and long-term software support in a compact design. Powered by the Tensor G3 processor, it features a bright 6.2-inch OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, a capable 50MP dual-camera system, and a 4575mAh battery with wireless charging. With seven years of Android updates, IP68 durability, and Google's advanced AI tools, the Pixel 8 is one of the best compact Android flagships available.
