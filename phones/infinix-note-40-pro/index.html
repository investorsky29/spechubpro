Expert Review

The Infinix Note 40 Pro is a premium mid-range smartphone that combines elegant design with flagship-inspired features. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G99 Ultimate processor, it delivers smooth gaming, multitasking, and everyday performance. Its stunning 6.78-inch curved AMOLED display supports a 120Hz refresh rate, producing vibrant colors, deep blacks, and fluid animations. The 108MP main camera with optical image stabilization captures detailed photos and stable videos in various lighting conditions. A 5000mAh battery supports ultra-fast 70W All-Round FastCharge 2.0 and convenient 20W MagCharge wireless charging. Additional features such as JBL-tuned dual stereo speakers, Active Halo AI lighting, NFC, an IR blaster, IP53 splash resistance, and Android 14 with XOS 14 make the Note 40 Pro one of the best premium 4G smartphones in its class.
